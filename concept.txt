Requirements of app:

Leaderboard:
Display Points, Games won, Nickname, games drawn, losses

Match tracker:
Takes 2 plyers, has winner selection with confirmation, records time and date of concluded match, score (score has ticker to go up or down, reset)

Game tracker: timer 30mins, player name 

Match History of player:
Shows who they competed against, the game, the score, the date and time

use cases:
add player (points field, game field)

start a match (competitive) (competitive takes 2 players and triggers start a match)

timer

view match history of a player

track 4 matches at a time
